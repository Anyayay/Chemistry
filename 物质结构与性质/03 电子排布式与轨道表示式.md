  $Fe^{3+}:[Ar]3d^5$
- $Cu:[Ar]3d^{10}4s^1$
  $Cu^{1+}:[Ar]3d^{10}$
  $Cu^{2+}:[Ar]3d^9$
- $Zn:[Ar]3d^{10}4s^2$
  $Zn^{1+}:[Ar]3d^{10}$
